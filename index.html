About Dogar Welfare Trust

Our mission

Dogar Welfare Trust works to uplift individuals and families through free medical outreach, education, and matrimonial support—guided by transparency, respect, and long-term community partnership.

What we focus on

We coordinate programs that remove barriers to sight, learning, and stable family life. Our affiliated initiatives operate with local volunteers and trusted partners so help reaches people quickly and fairly.
